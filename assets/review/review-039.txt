
Carbon Fiber Poles

2023-10-21

Carbon Fiber Poles offer significant advantages such as light swing weight for reduced arm fatigue and quicker, precise pole plants, as well as excellent stiffness for efficient power transfer. However, they require careful handling due to their susceptibility to sharp impacts and may need larger powder baskets for soft snow. These poles deliver performance benefits but come with inherent risks and require mindful use.
